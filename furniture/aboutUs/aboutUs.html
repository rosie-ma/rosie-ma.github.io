DESIGN

The first design ideas are developed through hand sketches, mood boards, 3D renderings, colour sketches and watercolours.
A design consultation is an opportunity to explore how you can turn your ideas into a reality. Whatever you want for your home, our skilled design consultants have the knowledge to make it happen, creating a solution that’s perfect for you and your lifestyle. The consultation is time to think big, explore the possibilities and define exactly what you want, in the comfort of your own home. We can work with photographs, technical drawings and renderings of your intended space; however, most of our clients prefer a personal visit from one of our designers, so we can really get a feel for your vision.
Fabrics, materials, budgets and technology will be discussed at this stage.
The initial design phase culminates in a presentation of full colour drawings or 3D renderings, along with wood, fabric, leathers or finishes samples.

×

CRAFTING

When you are completely happy with the final design, the piece will be put into production with some of the finest artisans in England. Our clients are invited to experience a workshop visit to see their design coming to life during the crafting phase.
We will closely work with any third parties involved with the project, such as interior designers, architects, builders or specialist consultants to ensure a smooth transition from the initial design through to delivery and installation.
We will oversee delivery to your space and installation, ensuring that your new furniture is correctly and safely situated.

×

COMMISSIONING

When you are happy with the initial designs and are ready to proceed, we will officially commission the project. Once commissioned we then provide full, detailed technical CAD drawings to be signed off and confirmed. We can also produce full size templates where required.

About Us

Harry G. Furniture based company specialising in the design, manufacture and installation of bespoke fitted furniture for your home.

Our company has over 25 years' experience within the industry giving us an unrivalled reputation of being able to offer you tailored furniture solutions for any room in your home.

From craftsmen to customer care we take great care of you every step of the way turning your dream into reality.

We specialise in bespoke kitchens, bedrooms, home studies, bathrooms and living room furniture. All designed and created with you to give you the perfect solution to compliment your home regardless of space or budget.

Our designs range from traditional to contemporary and as we use vast range of suppliers we can offer a solution to fit any space in your home. All of our solutions are fitted with soft closing systems to offer extra durability to your furniture.

Our service includes a free home visit to allow us to visualise what you require. Using our experience we can tailor a bespoke design to fit your home. We will bring brochures and samples of the products we use to allow you to see the quality for yourself. A full survey is carried out on your room and 3D Max design will be sent through with our no obligation quotation so you can see what the end result will look like before you buy.

Please contact us today to allow us to turn your desire into reality.
